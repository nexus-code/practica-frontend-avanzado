BeerFlix~ a beer moments tribute ~

This is a βeta application for the advanced Frontend module of Keepcoding's Full Stack course. You can consult a list of more than 80 amazing beers served through a curious API. You can make filters with the navbar search engine both by text and by year (2007-2019) of brewing. You can also express your comments and give "I like" to highlight your favorite. Be sure to check the best valueds beers.
